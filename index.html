The Bride and Groom

LACEY

Sarah is most thankful to her parents for the gift of two loving big brothers & five amazing role-model sisters. As the second-youngest, Sarah spent her childhood twirling & dancing around, putting on talent shows for her parents & grandparents. Her creative spirit and eye for design was established at a young age, as she highly disapproved of Mattel’s © furnishings and décor within her Barbie House. If Barbie needed an interior makeover (which was frequent) Sarah looked through Sunday newspaper ads & IKEA magazines for inspiration, drew the new contents by hand and placed them as backdrops in the Barbie house. After graduation from Mater Dei High School, Sarah explored the metropolitan life in San Francisco and graduated with a BS in Interior Design from San Francisco State University. Sarah is adding to her vows that she will forever allow a one-room “man-den” with it’s contents and décor subject to Bradley’s discretion.

PAULO

Born in Porto Alegre, Brazil, Paulo grew up in a loving family of four children. While growing up he spent most of his time playing sports, hanging out with his friends and playing games and RPGs. Paulo is a bit of a nerd, he was 6 years old when his family got their first computer. He always had an interest in computers and after serving an LDS mission in California Carlsbad, went to BYU-Idaho to get his degree in Computer Science. He has been working for Intel in Idaho Falls for 2 years now.

HOW WE MET

On New Year's Eve of 2015-2016, Paulo was in this party that Lacey's roommates were hosting. Paulo, seeing this cute and happy girl, started to chat with her and made a connection right away. The two had a lot of similar interests and then played a game of Mario Kart together. Later on the same night they set the first date and the rest is history - they've shared amazing adventures, and now are ready to share the rest of their lives together.

THE ENGAGEMENT

It was on May 4th, 2016 (Star Wars day was just [maybe] a coindidence 😃). Paulo called the principal of the school where Lacey works and planned that the principal would call Lacey into his office such that Paulo could sneak into her classroom. Everything went according to plan. Paulo sneaked into Lacey's classroom, handed WILL YOU MARRY ME posters to the children, then waited until she got back. When Lacey got back she saw the posters and was totally surprised! Paulo then dropped to his knees and popped the question. She smiled and said yes. All of that was captured in video as seen below ❤

DIRECTIONS

Northbound from San Diego

Take Interstate 5 North to the Villa de La Valle exit #36. Turn right (east) onto Via de La Valle, and proceed for 4 miles. After crossing La Gracia, turn left on Via de Santa Fe following the signs to Rancho Santa Fe. At the first stop sign, turn left onto Paseo Delicias and drive 2 blocks to a five way stop. Take a slight right onto Linea del Cielo and proceed for one block. The Inn is located on the left side of the road at 5951 Linea del Cielo.

Southbound from LA, Riverside & OC

Take Interstate 5 South to the Lomas Santa Fe exit #37. Turn left (east) onto Lomas Santa Fe, and proceed 4.5 miles. The road name will change to Linea Del Cielo. The Inn is located on the right side of the road at 5951 Linea del Cielo.

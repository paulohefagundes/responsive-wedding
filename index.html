Born in Porto Alegre, Brazil, Paulo grew up in a loving family of four children. While growing up he spent most of his time playing sports, hanging out with his friends and playing games and RPGs. HOW WE MET

On New Year's Eve of 2015 to 2016, Paulo was in this party that Lacey's roommates were hosting. Paulo, seeing this cute and happy girl, started to chat with Lacey and made a connection right away. The two soon found out they had a lot of similar interests and played a game of Mario Kart together that night. Later they've set their first date and the rest is history - they've shared amazing adventures, and now are ready to share the rest of their lives together.
